# **App Name**: FitStride

## Core Features:

- Progress Predictor AI: Leverages logged weight, calorie intake, and workout data to predict future weight changes, overall body progress, and estimated goal completion time. The tool's logic uses historical user data trends and statistical modeling to forecast potential outcomes, providing a personalized roadmap.
- Visual Progress Trends: Displays interactive graphs for tracking weight, calorie intake, and workout consistency. Users can view weekly and monthly trends with clear line or bar charts to visualize their journey and easily understand their progress over time.
- Daily Weight Log: Allows users to log their weight daily with an easy-to-use input interface. Provides a weekly summary with calculated averages and simple insights into fluctuations and overall progress over seven days, encouraging consistent tracking.
- Workout & Exercise Logger: Enables detailed logging of exercises, sets, repetitions, and weight lifted for strength and cardio sessions. Includes a library of pre-designed workout plans (e.g., Push/Pull/Legs) and maintains a comprehensive history of all completed sessions for easy review.
- Daily Diet & Calorie Tracker: Facilitates quick recording of daily food intake. Offers estimated calorie and protein counts for logged items, utilizing a simplified, performance-optimized database that includes common Indian food items to cater to local user needs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, muted forest green (#29A35E). This color signifies growth and vitality, creating a motivating yet calm user experience, and provides excellent contrast against a light background.
- Background color: An airy, highly desaturated pale green (#E7F7EF). This light hue enhances readability and provides a clean, unobtrusive canvas, ensuring the interface remains performance-friendly on low-to-mid-range devices.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet serene teal (#61CCCC). Analogous to the primary, this color stands out with higher saturation and brightness, used sparingly for calls-to-action, highlights, and interactive elements to draw attention without being overwhelming.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif. Its modern, neutral, and highly readable design ensures clarity for data-rich displays and contributes to a clean, performance-friendly user interface, suitable for both headlines and detailed text.
- Utilize simple, clean line-art icons that are consistent in style. Icons should be easily understandable metaphors for fitness activities, optimizing for quick recognition and reduced rendering load on mobile devices.
- Adopt a minimalistic, card-based layout with generous use of white space to enhance readability and reduce cognitive load. Key information and interactive elements will be prioritized, ensuring easy navigation with a clear bottom navigation bar and thumb-friendly button placement.
- Incorporate subtle, micro-animations for interactive feedback, such as state changes, form submissions, and data updates. Focus on performance-optimized transitions to maintain a smooth and responsive experience across all Android devices, avoiding heavy visual effects.
