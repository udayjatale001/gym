# MY GYM BUDDY: DISCIPLINE MODE

**My Gym Buddy** is a high-performance fitness "Discipline Engine" designed for athletes who treat their training like a mission. It moves beyond casual tracking to provide a professional, data-driven "Command Center" for physical transformation.

## 🚀 CORE PHILOSOPHY: DISCIPLINE MODE
The app utilizes an elite, high-contrast visual language (Absolute Black & Neon Green) to maintain focus and intensity. It is built around **30-Day Protocols** and **Daily Strides**.

## 📊 KEY FEATURES

### 1. THE ANALYTICS HUB & CALORIE STRIDE
- **Cumulative Energy Protocol**: A custom-engineered ring that sums your intake throughout the day (e.g., 100 + 500 = 600).
- **Overload Warning System**: If your intake exceeds your target, the ring activates a **Neon Red Overflow Layer**, signaling a breach in your energy protocol.
- **30-Day Energy History**: Accessible via the **🗓️ Elite Calendar Command**, this grid archives every day of your transformation cycle.

### 2. TRAINING COMMAND
- **Automated PPL Rotation**: The app intelligently cycles through **Push, Pull, and Legs** based on your start date, removing decision fatigue from your routine.
- **Session Heat Check**: Real-time estimation of volume and calorie burn for every workout.
- **Exercise Recall**: Instantly clone previous sessions to ensure progressive overload.

### 3. THE STRIDE SYSTEM
- **Recovery Stride (Sleep)**: Tracks deep rest and recovery status with a dedicated "Recovery Mode" UI.
- **Hydration Stride (Water)**: Quick-log 250ml intervals to secure your hydration goal.
- **Mobility Stride (Steps)**: Active tracking of daily movement with integrated kcal-burn estimation.

### 4. INTELLIGENT PROTOCOLS
- **Midnight Reset**: Automated system-wide reset at 12:00 AM to clear daily intake and mobility metrics while archiving final totals.
- **30-Day Diet Grid**: A consistency heatmap for tracking meal adherence (Taken vs. Skipped).
- **Market Trend Weight Analysis**: A sophisticated chart that visualizes body mass fluctuations like a high-end trading platform.

## 🛠 TECH STACK
- **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (Discipline Mode Theme)
- **UI Components**: Shadcn UI (Custom Glassmorphism)
- **Database**: Local-First Persistence with Firestore ready logic
- **AI Engine**: Genkit (Protocol Analysis)
